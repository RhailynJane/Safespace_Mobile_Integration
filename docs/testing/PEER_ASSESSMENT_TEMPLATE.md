## Evaluation Criteria Guide

### 1. Motivation and Engagement (1-3)

**3 - Excellent:**
- Consistently proactive and enthusiastic
- Takes initiative beyond assigned tasks
- Actively participates in all team discussions
- Shows genuine interest in project success

**2 - Acceptable:**
- Generally participates as expected
- Completes assigned work adequately
- Contributes to discussions when prompted
- Some inconsistency in engagement level

**1 - Needs Improvement:**
- Minimal participation in team activities
- Requires frequent reminders
- Appears disinterested or disengaged
- Limited contribution to team discussions

---

### 2. Timely Submission of Deliverables (1-3)

**3 - Excellent:**
- Consistently submits work ahead of deadlines
- Communicates proactively about timeline
- Never causes delays for the team
- Helps others meet their deadlines

**2 - Acceptable:**
- Generally meets deadlines
- Occasional minor delays with communication
- Mostly reliable with time commitments
- Needs occasional reminders

**1 - Needs Improvement:**
- Frequently misses deadlines
- Causes delays for team progress
- Poor communication about delays
- Requires constant follow-up

---

### 3. Effective Communication and Problem Solving (1-3)

**3 - Excellent:**
- Communicates clearly and professionally
- Responsive to messages within 24 hours
- Proactively raises issues and solutions
- Helps resolve team conflicts constructively

**2 - Acceptable:**
- Adequate communication most of the time
- Sometimes slow to respond
- Participates in problem solving when asked
- Basic conflict resolution skills

**1 - Needs Improvement:**
- Poor or inconsistent communication
- Rarely responds to messages
- Does not contribute to problem solving
- Avoids or escalates conflicts

---

### 4. Technical Contribution Quality (1-3)

**3 - Excellent:**
- Work is high quality and well-documented
- Code/tests are clean and maintainable
- Demonstrates strong technical skills
- Helps team members with technical challenges

**2 - Acceptable:**
- Work meets minimum requirements
- Generally functional with minor issues
- Basic technical competency
- Occasional errors requiring rework

**1 - Needs Improvement:**
- Work is incomplete or poor quality
- Frequent errors requiring team fixes
- Limited technical understanding
- Does not meet assignment standards

---

### 5. Collaboration and Teamwork (1-3)

**3 - Excellent:**
- Excellent team player, supportive of others
- Shares knowledge and resources freely
- Adapts well to different working styles
- Builds positive team morale

**2 - Acceptable:**
- Works adequately with team members
- Shares information when requested
- Generally cooperative
- Maintains professional relationships

**1 - Needs Improvement:**
- Difficult to work with
- Does not share information or resources
- Negative impact on team morale
- Prefers to work in isolation
